Portable Arduino mp3 PlayerTop

For the first semester, we had to find a guide for some sort of project that interested us online. It should have eben a faitly complete guide that would walk us through step by step to the final project. The guide i chose was one on how to make a portable mp3 player with an arduino, micro SD card mp3 module, and OLED display. It has 3 buttons for navigating and controlling the mp3 player, a rechargeable li-ion battery, and and Arduino pro mini for controlling everything.
